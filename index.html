Description

Amass is a fast pace game that requires the player to know where all obstacles are at all times. Player must amass as many green power squares as possible without colliding with any Red Balls “The Threat”.

There are four power balls to help or hurt you during the game: Yellow Ball "The Divider" Destroys half the balls on screen Black Ball "The Decelerator " Slows you down for 5 seconds Purple Ball "The Accelerator" Speeds you up for 5 seconds White Ball "The Annihilator" Destroys all balls on screen

How To Play

Timing is everything. Knowing when to go after the power square and when to wait is to key to amassing as much as possible.

How to Play Use the joystick at the bottom of the device to control the blue square. Amass as many green power squares as possible. Avoid all threats.
